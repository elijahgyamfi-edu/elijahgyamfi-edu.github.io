About Me

I am a passionate educator with experience in teaching and applying technology to enhance teaching and learning.

Teaching — Mathematics

I am an experienced mathematics educator with more than five years of classroom and tutoring experience across Algebra, Geometry, Precalculus, Calculus, and introductory Statistics. I foster a growth mindset by creating a learning environment where students can try, fail safely, and receive timely, actionable feedback.

My teaching blends clear conceptual instruction with active-learning strategies — problem clinics, collaborative tasks, and low-stakes formative checks — to make abstract ideas accessible and build student confidence. I design scaffolded curricula and aligned assessments, run focused exam-prep workshops, mentor competition teams, and maintain an open-source problem bank used by colleagues.

Areas of focus: curriculum design, formative assessment, exam preparation, and inclusive pedagogy. Contact me to request syllabi, workshop materials, or a CV.
